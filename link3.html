How Dance Affects the Audience

 Dance has a very powerful way of moving people. As dancers, we are able to tell a story with our bodies and emotions. Oftentimes, audience members will feel the connection with the performer, even though they do not know each other personally. A song can potentially bring back a million memories for someone. It could be anything from a love song, a favorite song, or a song that reminds them of a difficult time. Audiences are usually filled with those who have studied dance, as well as those who have not. Regardless of your dance background, there is definitely a piece that will personally touch your heart.
